Your Personal Safety Network

FlareRed syncs with your phone’s contacts, allowing you to build a trusted circle of friends and family for emergencies. Add or remove contacts easily from the settings tab, ensuring your safety network is always up to date. The app integrates seamlessly with your device’s notification system, so alerts and real-time location updates reach your group instantly, no matter where they are. Real-Time Location Sharing

FlareRed enables precise, real-time tracking through advanced geolocation APIs, like Leaflet, HTML Geolocation, and Socket.io. When you activate a Flare—an emergency alert signal—a secure, private link is sent to your selected contacts, allowing them to view your exact location on an interactive map. The flare system works seamlessly, ensuring your safety group is instantly notified. Each flare also comes with time-stamped updates, so your network can track any changes in your movement. The flare technology is optimized to use minimal battery while maintaining continuous monitoring for prolonged safety.
